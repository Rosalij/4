About Us

The shop is owned by Elias Morren, a lifelong collector of forgotten novels, banned essays, and hand-bound curiosities. Elias is rarely seen on the shop floor, but his presence lingers in every carefully chosen title and every handwritten note tucked between the shelves.

The staff is a close-knit group of readers and researchers, each with their own specialties—lost poetry, obscure philosophy, midnight fiction, and books that were never meant to be found. They are always happy to guide visitors, though they prefer quiet conversations and thoughtful recommendations over hurried questions. If you know where to look, you’ll know how to reach us.
